Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our psychological services and how we can help you.

How do I know if I need to see a psychologist?

If you're experiencing persistent feelings of stress, anxiety, sadness, or difficulty coping with daily life, consulting a psychologist can provide valuable guidance and support.

What should I expect during my first session?

During your first session, your psychologist will ask questions to understand your concerns and goals. This is a safe space to share your thoughts and feelings, with no judgment.

Are my sessions confidential?

Yes, all sessions are confidential. We adhere to strict ethical guidelines and privacy laws to ensure your information is protected, except in cases required by law.

How long does therapy usually last?

The length of therapy varies for each individual and depends on your specific goals and needs. Your psychologist will discuss a plan with you during your sessions.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, we offer online sessions for clients who prefer virtual consultations. This is a convenient way to access therapy from the comfort of your home.

What types of issues can a psychologist help with?

Our psychologists specialize in various areas, including anxiety, depression, stress management, relationship issues, trauma, and personal development.
